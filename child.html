About Pediatrics

Pediatrics is a specialty of medicine that focuses on the health and medical treatment of babies, children, and adolescents from birth to the age of 18. Pediatrics is concerned not only about the natural illness of the child but also long-term effects on quality of life, disability, and survival. The word pediatrics means healer of children. It is derived from two Greek words, “pais” which means child, and “iatros” which means doctor or healer.

uring the pediatric age, children very frequently suffer from illnesses like respiratory conditions, worm infestation, urinary tract infections, nutritional problems. Pediatricians suggest the required medicines, nutrition for the child and take care of the vaccinations that have been taken at that age. The father of pediatrics is Abraham Jacobi. In India, pediatrics began in 1928. Dr. George Coelho is the Father of Indian Pediatrics. Pediatrics is a new medical specialty, developing in the mid-19th century.
