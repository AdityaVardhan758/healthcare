About Orthopedic

Orthopedics or orthopedic surgery is a branch of surgery that deals with the conditions of the musculoskeletal system. It includes bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, muscles, and nerves.

Orthopedic surgeons are doctors who specialize in this field. They use surgical and nonsurgical ways to treat musculoskeletal trauma, spine diseases, sports injuries, degenerative diseases, infections, tumors, and congenital disorders. These involve a diagnosis of your injury, treatment with medication, exercise, casting, surgery, or other options.
