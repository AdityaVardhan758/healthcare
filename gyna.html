About Gynaecology

Gynecology deals with any illness concerning the reproductive organs like the uterus, fallopian tubes, cervix, ovaries, and vagina. A gynecologist also treats the issues in the bowel, bladder, and urinary system are all related to the female reproductive organs. A gynecologist also treats the prolapse of the pelvis organs, which is a usual condition present in postmenopausal women with weakened pelvic muscles that cannot support the uterus or bladder properly.
