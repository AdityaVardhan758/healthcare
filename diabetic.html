About Diabetology

Diabetology is the significant branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of Diabetes Mellitus known as Diabetes, Blood Sugar. Diabetology is a sub-specialty of endocrinology. Diabetes is a disease in which glucose does not reach the cells, causing them to go without food. Diabetology deals with all types of diabetes. Diabetes could be due to the low levels of insulin in the blood or the reduced ability of the body to use insulin. Insulin is a hormone that is secreted by the pancreas, which in turn helps our cells to absorb glucose. High levels of blood sugar could lead to serious complications to the heart, eye, foot, or kidney.

Diabetologists are doctors who specialize in diabetes diagnosis, management, and treatment. Diabetologists treat three basic kinds of diabetes: type 1, type 2, and gestational diabetes, as well as other less prevalent diabetic diseases.
